# Privacy Policy for Claude Outline Navigator & Export

**Last Updated: January 2024**

## Overview
Claude Outline Navigator & Export ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed to enhance your experience on Claude.ai by providing conversation navigation and export features.

## Data Collection
**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What We Don't Collect:
- Personal identification information
- Conversation content from Claude.ai
- Browsing history
- User activity or behavior data
- Any data outside of Claude.ai
- No analytics or tracking data

### What We Store Locally:
The extension stores only the following data **locally in your browser**:
- User preferences (e.g., preferred export format)
- Outline display settings

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any server
- Is not shared with third parties
- Can be cleared by removing the extension

## Permissions Explained

### activeTab
Required to read the current Claude.ai conversation when you click the extension icon, enabling outline generation and export features.

### storage
Used to save your preferences locally in your browser for a consistent experience.

### Host Permission (https://claude.ai/*)
Required to access and interact with Claude.ai pages. The extension only works on Claude.ai and does not access any other websites.

## How the Extension Works
1. When you click the extension icon on Claude.ai, it reads the visible conversation
2. It generates an outline structure locally in your browser
3. When you export, the file is created locally and downloaded to your device
4. No data is sent to any external server

## Third-Party Services
This extension does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Data Security
All processing happens locally in your browser. We do not have access to your data because we never receive it.

## Children's Privacy
This extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Your Rights
You can:
- Uninstall the extension at any time
- Clear local storage through browser settings
- Contact us with privacy questions
